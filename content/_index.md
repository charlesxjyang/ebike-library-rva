> Read about us in [Charlottesville Tomorrow](https://www.cvilletomorrow.org/wanna-try-an-e-bike-josh-carp-will-lend-you-his/)!

We're a free e-bike lending library serving the Charlottesville area.
You can visit and and test-ride any of our e-bikes, or take one home for
up to a week at a time. You can sign up to borrow an e-bike using our
[sign-up form](https://forms.gle/ZykVSUUp2KPBHxfv5), or by emailing us
at hi@ebikelibrarycville.org.
